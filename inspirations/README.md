# inspirations/

Open-ended project inspirations for the hackathon and future contributors.

These are not assignments, requirements, or limits. They are starting points for
thinking. Contributors are encouraged to define their own approach, combine
directions, or propose something entirely different if it advances ethical
menstrual health research infrastructure.

The only shared constraints are:

- Use synthetic, fictional, open, or approved aggregate resources in public work.
- Do not expose real participant-level data.
- Document assumptions, data flow, limitations, and governance choices.
- Keep the work reusable for others.

Start with `project_inspirations.md`.
