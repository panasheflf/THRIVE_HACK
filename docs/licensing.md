data so contributors can reuse each part appropriately.

Commons Attribution 4.0 International. See `LICENSE-DOCS.md`.

These materials may be copied, translated, adapted, and reused with attribution.

Synthetic datasets and generated synthetic fixtures in `synthetic-data/` are
released under CC0 1.0 Universal. See `synthetic-data/LICENSE`.

Synthetic data is fictional. It must not be represented as real participant
data, clinical evidence, or epidemiological evidence.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise in the contribution, contributions are
accepted under the license that applies to the file or directory being changed.

Do not contribute third-party content unless its license permits inclusion in
this repository and attribution requirements are documented.
