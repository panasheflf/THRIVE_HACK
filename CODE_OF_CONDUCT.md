# Code Of Conduct

This project is about menstrual health research infrastructure. Contributors
should treat each other, research participants, community partners, and affected
communities with respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Use inclusive language.
- Respect cultural context and community knowledge.
- Avoid stigmatizing or sensational language around menstrual health.
- Protect privacy and confidentiality.
- Document assumptions and limitations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory behavior
- Sharing real participant data in public spaces
- Re-identification attempts
- Unsupported clinical claims
- Extractive or disrespectful framing of communities
- Publishing secrets, credentials, or private partner material

## Reporting

Project maintainers should provide a public reporting contact before launch.
